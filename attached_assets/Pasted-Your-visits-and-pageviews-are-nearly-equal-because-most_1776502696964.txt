Your **visits and pageviews are nearly equal because most users are only loading one page per visit**. Cloudflare’s web analytics defines these as separate metrics, so a near 1:1 ratio usually means low session depth, not a tracking bug .

## What the ratio means
- **Visits** count sessions.
- **Pageviews** count page loads.
- If pageviews are only slightly above visits, users are mostly landing and leaving, or they are hitting just one page and exiting quickly .

## Why this can happen
- You may be getting a lot of **single-page sessions** from search or direct traffic.
- Some visits may be **bot-like or prefetch-like** behavior depending on how the site is accessed, which can make pageview patterns look flatter.
- If your site is an SPA or has heavy client-side navigation, analytics can also behave differently depending on how the beacon is configured .
